First Dashboard

This first sketch shows weather in a clock-like fashion. The different temperatures are represented by warmer and cooler colors, and they are arranged around the circle on a gradient that shows each hour, and minute as a fraction of a ring, starting at the red line, which represents the current time. THe hours are represented by the outer circle and the minutes are represented by the inner circle.


Second Dashboard

This sketch shows the high and low temperatures of the week. Each vertical pair of circle is a high and a low for a single day, the first row being the current day. The week of highs and lows is centered around a middle axis which represents the avg. weekly temperature.
