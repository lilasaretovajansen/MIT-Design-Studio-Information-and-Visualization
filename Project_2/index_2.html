First Dashboard

Boston, Ma

In this first sketch, the bar graph represents the temperature range for each day in the week. The dotted line shows the average weekly temperature, and the little orange rect on the first bar shows the current temperature. Below the graph, each day has a weather icon for the general daily weather. Finally, a daily summary is given at the bottom, alongside an arrow that shows weather the temperature is currently increasing or decreasing.
